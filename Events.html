Daydream

Hack Club Daydream is an event where high school students explore creativity and technology by building projects, joining workshops, and collaborating with peers. It provides a supportive environment to learn coding, share ideas, and showcase innovative projects.

Summer Of Making

Hack Club Summer of Making (SOM) is a global summer program that encourages high school students to create personal tech projects such as apps, games, websites, or hardware prototypes. It provides a structured environment to learn new skills and explore creativity over the summer.

The program also offers mentorship, community support, and sometimes funding for projects, helping students gain confidence, complete meaningful projects, and connect with like-minded peers around the world.
