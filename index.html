ABOUT US

Home to a community of tech enthusiasts, TeshCrafters offers a platform for hands-on learning, workshops, coding competitions, and collaborative projects. Whether you're looking to develop your programming skills, work with hardware, or network with other innovators, TeshCrafters has something for everyone. TeshCrafters is proud to be a part of the global Hack Club movement – a worldwide network of high school coding clubs and makerspaces. Hack Club empowers students to be makers and leaders by providing resources, guidance, and a collaborative community. By joining TeshCrafters, you are also connecting with Hack Clubbers from around the world, sharing projects, participating in international hackathons, and accessing a wealth of open source knowledge. Learn more at hackclub.com.
